# disk-guard — Guide

Disk space emergency response and prevention.

## Triggers

- "disk full", "out of space", "no space left"
- Apps crashing, WiFi issues, Tailscale disconnects

## Actions

1. Quick diagnostics (df, du)
2. Safe reclaim (caches, temp files, logs)
3. Deep scan (large files, duplicates)
4. Prevention (monitoring setup)
